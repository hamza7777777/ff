ABOUT US


WELCOME TO BASALT



BASALT engineering consultant is proud to present itself as a dynamic company، the core of the company is creative experienced engineers, their fundamentals are creativity, hardworking and above all experience. BASALT is a leading company in UAE market in the field of, architecture, interior, MEP, supervision, environment consultancy and project management with architects experience in projects range from private villas, palaces, residential towers, mosques، factories، sports facilities، hotels to huge shopping malls. BASALT was founded in 2013 in Abu Dhabi. BASALT consider quality the main pillar of all work to maintain a long and productive relationship with clients through achieving project success within time،budget، quality to international codes، standards and client satisfaction. BASALT reputation is based on the creativity of its leading architects and their ability to translate clients’ needs into innovative design solutions.
